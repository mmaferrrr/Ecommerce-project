WELCOME TO ART BY NOLQUI

Where creativity becomes art in the heart of Charlotte, North Carolina. With over a decade of experience, we take pride in transforming nails into stunning works of art.

At Art by Nolqui, every stroke of polish is infused with passion and precision. We specialize in bringing your nail dreams to life, whether it's elegant and understated or bold and extravagant. From classic French tips to avant-garde designs, we cater to every style and occasion.

SAFETY

Safety and Sanitation are a priority when it comes to nails. Here in Art by Nolqui we ensure our implements, equipment, and electrical instruments are always thoroughly cleaned and subjected to an approved sanitizing and disinfecting process after each client.

Recommended Products for Aftercare

Proper aftercare is essential to maintaining nail's health and longevity. One often overlooked but crucial aspect of aftercare is cuticle oil.

It hydrates and nourishes the cuticles, keeping them soft and preventing painful hangnails and dry, cracked skin. When applied regularly, cuticle oil promotes healthy nail growth and strengthens the nails, reducing the risk of breakage.
